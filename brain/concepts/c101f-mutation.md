## Concept Note: C101F Mutation in PfCRT Dd2 — *Plasmodium falciparum*

**Entity:** C101F point mutation in PfCRT (Plasmodium falciparum chloroquine resistance transporter), Dd2 genetic background

---

### Summary

C101F substitution in PfCRT Dd2 isoform associates with **collateral hypersensitivity** — increased susceptibility — across three mechanistically distinct antimalarials: **artemisinin**, **quinine**, and **amodiaquine**. All three effects observed *in vitro* vs. Dd2 without C101F.

---

### Claims

| Drug | Direction | Setting | Certainty | Evidence Grade |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Artemisinin | ↑ susceptibility | in vitro | Moderate | Expert opinion |
| Quinine | ↑ susceptibility | in vitro | Moderate | Expert opinion |
| Amodiaquine | ↑ susceptibility | in vitro | Moderate | Expert opinion |

**Comparator:** PfCRT Dd2 isoform without C101F  
**Source:** PMID:28487425 — *"A Variant PfCRT Isoform Can Contribute to Plasmodium falciparum Resistance to the First-Line Partner Drug Piperaquine"*

---

### Mechanistic Context

PfCRT is a digestive vacuole transporter; mutations alter drug efflux/sequestration dynamics. C101F sits in transmembrane domain 2. Dd2 background already carries CQ-resistance mutations (K76T + others); C101F appears to partially reverse transport function for these drugs — a fitness trade-off accompanying piperaquine resistance adaptation.

---

### Gaps & Caveats

- **No IC₅₀ values** reported in claims — effect size quantification absent
- Evidence grade **expert opinion** (not RCT/controlled experiment with full statistical reporting) — moderate certainty ceiling
- **In vitro only** — no clinical or *in vivo* pharmacokinetic context
- C101F role may be **epistatic** — effect magnitude likely background-dependent beyond Dd2

---

### Clinical Relevance Signal

Collateral hypersensitivity to artemisinin + partner drugs (quinine, amodiaquine) suggests C101F-carrying parasites may paradoxically retain treatment sensitivity despite being part of a piperaquine-resistant lineage. Potential **partner drug rescue** scenario, but requires in vivo validation.

**Status:** All claims pending review. Do not use for clinical guidance without further corroboration.
