### Synthesis

AL replaced both conventional first-line regimens (CQ+SP, AQ+SP) in PNG based on superior clinical treatment failure rates. Underlying driver: in vitro sensitivity data showing *P. falciparum* resistance to chloroquine and sulphadoxine-pyrimethamine components, rendering non-artemisinin combinations clinically inadequate in endemic PNG settings.
