## Concept Note — PfCRT Dd2+C101F Isoform

**Entity:** PfCRT Dd2+C101F isoform · *Plasmodium falciparum*
**Source:** PMID:28487425 · **As of:** 2026-05-09

---

### Core Biology

PfCRT (Plasmodium falciparum chloroquine resistance transporter) is a digestive vacuole membrane protein. Dd2 haplotype carries established resistance mutations (incl. K76T). C101F is an additional point mutation — cysteine→phenylalanine at position 101 — conferring an expanded resistance profile beyond canonical Dd2.

---

### Resistance Claims (×2)

| Drug | Setting | Comparator | Certainty | Evidence Grade |
|------|---------|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| Blasticidin | in vitro | Dd2 without C101F | Moderate | Expert opinion |
| Amantadine | in vitro | Dd2 without C101F | Moderate | Expert opinion |

Both claims: Dd2+C101F outperforms parental Dd2 in surviving drug pressure. No IC₅₀/EC₅₀ values captured yet — effect size fields null.

---

### Synthesis

C101F augments Dd2 transport function, broadening substrate recognition to include structurally distinct compounds (blasticidin = aminoglycoside-class antibiotic; amantadine = adamantane antiviral). Both used experimentally as selectable markers or growth inhibitors in *P. falciparum* lab work — clinical relevance indirect, but mechanistic signal is real.

**Key implication:** Single point mutation on an already-resistant backbone extends cross-resistance to chemically unrelated scaffolds → supports a "promiscuous efflux" model for PfCRT variants.

---

### Gaps / Caveats

- No quantitative effect sizes — both claims lack IC₅₀ shift values
- Evidence grade = expert opinion (not RCT/controlled assay series)
- In vitro only — no in vivo or clinical correlation
- Status: `pending_review` — treat as provisional

---

**Bottom line:** Dd2+C101F = gain-of-function CRT variant. C101F mutation alone drives novel resistance to blasticidin + amantadine vs. Dd2 background. Mechanistic interest high; clinical weight low until quantified.
