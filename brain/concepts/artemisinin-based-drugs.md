## Concept Note: Artemisinin-Based Drug Resistance in *Plasmodium falciparum*

**Entity:** Artemisinin-based drugs
**Source:** PMID:36303209 | *Apicoplast ribosomal protein S10-V127M enhances artemisinin resistance of a Kelch13 transgenic Plasmodium falciparum*
**Claims synthesized:** 3 | **Certainty:** Moderate (all) | **Evidence grade:** Expert opinion

---

### Core Narrative

Resistance to artemisinin-based drugs in *P. falciparum* operates as a **layered mutational system**, not a single-gene switch. Three claims from the same study establish a resistance hierarchy:

| Genetic background | Comparator | Resistance phenotype |
|---|---|---|
| k13-C580Y alone | Wild-type 3D7 | Elevated RSA₀₋₃ₕ survival |
| k13-C580Y + arps10-V127M | k13-C580Y alone | Further increased RSA₀₋₃ₕ survival |
| k13-C580Y + fd-D193Y + arps10-V127M | k13-C580Y alone | Further increased RSA₀₋₃ₕ survival |

**k13-C580Y** is the gatekeeper — the canonical kelch-domain mutation that initiates resistance. **arps10-V127M** (apicoplast ribosomal protein S10) acts as a resistance *enhancer*: it boosts RSA survival on top of k13-C580Y and appears to contribute both alone and in combination with fd-D193Y.

---

### Mechanistic Implications

- arps10-V127M is an **apicoplast-compartment modifier**, pointing to ribosomal/translational fidelity in the apicoplast as a resistance amplifier — distinct from the canonical PI3K pathway targeted by k13
- The additive effect of fd-D193Y + arps10-V127M suggests **epistatic cooperation** among background mutations, which may explain field observations where k13 mutations alone don't fully predict clinical failure

---

### Gaps & Cautions

- **Effect sizes absent** — RSA survival rates, CIs, and mg/kg dose data not captured; quantitative comparison between tiers impossible from these claims alone
- **No population/regional data** — cannot link to specific transmission zones or patient demographics
- **Evidence grade: expert opinion** — likely transgenic parasite lab models, not clinical cohorts; generalizability to field isolates uncertain
- All claims **pending review** — treat as provisional

---

### Clinical Relevance

Surveillance programs tracking only k13 mutations **undercount resistance potential**. Co-occurring arps10-V127M (and fd-D193Y) should be monitored as resistance-potentiating companions, particularly in Southeast Asian contexts where C580Y is prevalent. ACT partner drug efficacy becomes more critical as background mutations accumulate.
