## Concept Note: Flow Cytometric Assay in Ring-Stage Survival Assay

**Entity:** Flow cytometric assay
**Source:** PMID:24867976 | *Flow cytometry-based analysis of artemisinin-resistant Plasmodium falciparum in the ring-stage survival assay*

---

### Core Claims

**1. Supersession of light microscopy** *(Certainty: High)*

Flow cytometric assay replaces light microscopy (Giemsa-stained thin blood smear) for viable parasite counting in the ring-stage survival assay (RSA). Key differentiator: counts **250,000 erythrocytes per assay** vs. the limited throughput of manual microscopy. Applicable across research laboratory and field settings.

**2. Standardization recommendation** *(Certainty: Moderate)*

Assay recommends RSA data standardization across research groups in both laboratory and field settings, with light microscopy as the implicit legacy comparator. Basis: expert opinion — no formal RCT or comparative trial data underpins the recommendation.

---

### Synthesis

| Dimension | Signal |
