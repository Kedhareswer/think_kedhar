## Synthesis

Three layered claims, all same paper, building one mechanistic picture:

### Layer 1 — k13-C580Y as baseline resistance allele
*P. falciparum* k13-C580Y variant resists artemisinin vs wild-type 3D7, measured by RSA₀₋₃ₕ survival. Establishes C580Y as the genetic scaffold on which further mutations act.

### Layer 2 — arps10-V127M as resistance enhancer
Adding arps10-V127M (apicoplast ribosomal protein S10) to k13-C580Y background further increases RSA₀₋₃ₕ survival vs k13-C580Y alone. arps10-V127M is not standalone — potentiates existing kelch13-driven resistance.

### Layer 3 — Double-companion mutation (fd-D193Y + arps10-V127M)
Combining fd-D193Y (ferredoxin) with arps10-V127M on k13-C580Y background amplifies resistance further vs k13-C580Y alone. Additive or synergistic companion-mutation effect.
