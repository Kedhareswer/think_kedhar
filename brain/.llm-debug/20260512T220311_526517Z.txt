Artemisinin-based combination therapy (ACT) is established first-line treatment for uncomplicated *P. falciparum* malaria in outpatient settings (PMID:38321292, certainty: **high**). Both standard ACT regimens effective; DHA-PPQ outperforms AL:

Source: PMID:34952573 — outpatient RCT, three Ugandan sentinel sites.
