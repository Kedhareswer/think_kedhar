## Overview

Pediatric malaria pharmacotherapy spans prevention ([[rts-s-as01]] vaccine), radical cure (8-aminoquinolines), and severe-disease empirical cover. Age-based eligibility and contraindications dominate prescribing decisions. Co-infection risk in severe pediatric malaria drives empirical antibacterial use [c:c09b8c11].

## Indication (Why is this medication prescribed?)

- Prevention of [[plasmodium-falciparum]] malaria in [[sub-saharan-africa]] — [[rts-s-as01]] vaccine recommended from age ≥5 months in moderate-to-high transmission regions [c:f1c540dd] *(pending review)*
