# Artemether-Lumefantrine

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin combination therapy (ACT)
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene endoperoxide (artemether) + aryl amino alcohol (lumefantrine)
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Fixed-dose oral ACT combining fast-acting artemether with longer-acting lumefantrine. Used as first-line treatment for [[uncomplicated-falciparum-malaria]] and other [[plasmodium]] species across endemic and imported (traveler) settings, including pregnancy and pediatric populations [c:abfc416f][c:c42adcd6]. Also studied for [[post-discharge-malaria-chemoprevention]] in pediatric [[severe-anaemia]] [c:15a79d74].
