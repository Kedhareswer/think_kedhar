# Tafenoquine

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Radical-cure agent (anti-relapse)
**Drug class:** 8-aminoquinoline
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Tafenoquine is an 8-aminoquinoline antimalarial used as single-dose radical cure to prevent [[plasmodium-vivax-recurrence]] by clearing hepatic hypnozoites. Individual-patient-data meta-analysis supports a dose-response relationship for recurrence prevention in adults [c:4461ec93].

## Indication (Why is this medication prescribed?)

- Radical cure of [[plasmodium-vivax-malaria]] to prevent recurrence in adults [c:4461ec93] (evidence_grade: meta_analysis).
