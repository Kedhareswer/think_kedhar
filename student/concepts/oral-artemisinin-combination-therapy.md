# Oral Artemisinin Combination Therapy

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin combination therapy (ACT)
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene endoperoxide + partner antimalarial
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Oral artemisinin combination therapy (ACT) pairs a fast-acting artemisinin derivative with a longer-acting partner drug. UK guidelines position oral ACT as a treatment option for imported [[non-falciparum-malaria]] in travellers presenting in non-endemic settings, as an alternative to [[chloroquine]] (pending review) [c:540756c3] [c:ef1f9fb3] [c:cc23c153].
