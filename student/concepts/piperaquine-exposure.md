## Overview

Long-acting bisquinoline antimalarial. Partner drug in dihydroartemisinin-piperaquine (DHA-PQ). Long terminal half-life drives post-treatment prophylaxis. Used for [[uncomplicated-falciparum-malaria]] treatment and intermittent preventive treatment in pregnancy ([[ipt-p]]). Current claim set covers exposure–outcome links in pregnancy only.
