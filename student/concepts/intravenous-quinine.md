# Quinine (intravenous)

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Cinchona alkaloid

## Overview

Intravenous quinine — parenteral cinchona alkaloid for severe/complicated falciparum malaria when artesunate unavailable. Inpatient-only, drip infusion. Second-line vs IV artesunate in UK guidelines [c:ce5df424] (pending review).
