# Dihydroartemisinin-piperaquine

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin combination therapy (ACT)
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene endoperoxide (dihydroartemisinin) + bisquinoline (piperaquine)
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Fixed-dose ACT combining short-acting [[dihydroartemisinin]] with long-acting [[piperaquine]]. Monthly dosing studied as intermittent preventive treatment in pregnancy (IPTp) for [[falciparum-malaria]] in endemic settings, with prolonged piperaquine half-life supporting chemoprevention between doses (pending review).

## Indication (Why is this medication prescribed?)

- Prevention of [[malaria-in-pregnancy]] — HIV-uninfected pregnant adolescents/adults in second/third trimester, endemic regions (Uganda data) [c:04d9bb43] (pending review)
