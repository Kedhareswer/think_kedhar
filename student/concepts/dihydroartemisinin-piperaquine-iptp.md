# Dihydroartemisinin-Piperaquine (IPTp)

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin combination therapy (ACT)
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene endoperoxide (dihydroartemisinin) + bisquinoline (piperaquine)
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Fixed-dose ACT used as intermittent preventive treatment in pregnancy (IPTp) in malaria-endemic settings. Investigational/programmatic alternative to sulfadoxine-pyrimethamine IPTp in [[sub-saharan-africa]] for preventing and treating [[plasmodium-falciparum]] infection during second and third trimesters.
