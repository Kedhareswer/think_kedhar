# Intravenous Artesunate

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin derivative (parenteral)
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene lactone endoperoxide
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Parenteral artemisinin derivative. First-line for severe/complicated malaria across *P. falciparum* and *P. knowlesi*. Replaces IV quinine in most guidelines. Inpatient use only. Transition to oral ACT once tolerated.
