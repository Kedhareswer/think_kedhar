# Intramuscular Artemether

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin derivative
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene lactone endoperoxide
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

Intramuscular artemether is an oil-based artemisinin derivative used for [[severe-malaria]] when parenteral [[artesunate]] is unavailable. Evidence supports faster parasitological and clinical recovery vs [[quinine]] in children, and lower mortality vs quinine in adults. Pediatric mortality benefit vs quinine not established. Recommended as fallback parenteral option in inpatient endemic settings (pending review) [c:9ff1e342].
