# Artemisinin Combination Therapies (ACT)

**Therapeutic category:** Antimalarial
**Drug group:** Artemisinin combination therapy
**Drug class:** Sesquiterpene endoperoxide + partner antimalarial
**Controlled substance:** No

## Overview

ACT pairs fast-acting artemisinin derivative with longer-acting partner drug. Frontline regimen for uncomplicated malaria in endemic settings. Combination delays resistance vs monotherapy. Partner drug clears residual parasites after artemisinin half-life ends.
