# Artemisinin Resistance in P. falciparum

Partial resistance, driven by Kelch13 mutations, now documented across SE Asia, sub-Saharan Africa, and India. Resistance is not monolithic.

## Synthesis

P. falciparum has evolved partial resistance to artemisinin (ART), driven by distinct genetic loci and at least two independent cellular survival pathways. [c:a4f2b8c1]

Why it's waiting for you
The system held this finding back for three reasons. Any one of them on its own would be enough to require a human review:
  • The finding comes from a single cohort study (researchers tracked one group of patients over time). Cohort studies are useful but weaker than randomised trials.
  • The researchers themselves rated their confidence as low — they're flagging it as a signal worth watching, not a settled conclusion.
  • The knowledge base has no other studies yet that either support or contradict this finding for West Africa. There's nothing to corroborate it.
